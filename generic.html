Training Philosophy

I’m a strong believer that for the majority of the population who wants to get into shape and become healthier, a well structured resistance training routine is the most optimal path to success. For the majority of people who get into fitness, their major goal usually boils down to looking good and feeling good, and this is best achieved by following a well structured resistance training routine that focuses on the major compound movements and improving strength over time. It doesn’t matter if you’re young or old, male or female, it works for everyone and is extremely safe and healthy. Many people are intimidated by weight lifting, but studies have shown that starting a resistance training program even at the age of 80 years old produces enourmous benefits.

In order to improve the way you look, you will want to improve your body composition. Body composition is the ratio of fat to lean mass that your body consists of and is what determines how you look. Those who have an athletic physique have a body which consists of a higher muscle to fat ratio. The most optimal way to improve body composition is to follow a resistance training program and proper dieting protocols. Progressing on a well structured resistance training routine will guarantee that your body’s fat to muscle ratio improves on a consistent basis, giving you that lean look that everyone is after. This is the fastest way to improve your physique; you will feel healthier and stronger, and you’ll look the part too!
