Training Philosophy

My philosophy on fitness revolves around resistance training and utilizing it's principals to create a stronger, healthier and better looking physique. I combine the training styles of power lifters and bodybuilders (sometimes called "powerbuilding") in order to illicit optimal progress for the drug free lifter. If your goal is to change your physique for the better while getting healthier, a well structured resistance training routine is the most optimal path to achieving your goals and is far superior to a cardio or conditioning only approach.

Resistance training can be performed safely by those of all ages and provides incredible health benefits that go far beyond just making you stronger. Resistance training helps maintain healthy bones, muscle tissue and ligaments, has disease preventing benefits and provides a great way to burn a lot of calories helping you maintain a healthy body composition.

For those who know a little more about strength training I'd like to briefly mention that my training style is influenced by the conjugate method and utilizes some of it's principals, namely high exercise selection and exercise rotation, mixed with undulated periodization. I also utilize both percentage based training and RPE based training in my routines.

Dieting Philosophy

No more forcing yourself to eat foods you don't enjoy all in the name of eating “clean”! My approach to dieting utilizes "If It Fits Your Macros" or IIFYM for short. With this approach you are allowed to eat any foods that you want as long as it fits into your daily calorie and macronutrient goals.

Eating healthy is something that everyone should strive to do, but a huge misconception in the fitness industry is that you must eat ONLY healthy foods in order to change your physique and get into shape, when in truth, weight gain and weight loss is determined by how MUCH you eat, not WHAT you eat. When you understand this, your ability to adhere to a diet becomes infinitely easier and long term success is almost a guarantee!
